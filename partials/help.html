How do I use Connect?

  • Create an account with us using an email address and password.
  • You will be introduced to your user profile, which contains all the events that you have previously shown interest in.
  • If You're Looking to Hire:
    1. To create a listing, simply select “Find Workers” from the home page or menu bar.
    2. Fill out a title for your listing, and choose between a volunteer or paid job.
    3. Finish filling in the rest of the listing details and click the "Submit" button
    4. Wait for potential workers to contact you
  • If You're Looking for Opportunities:
    1. Select “Find Opportunities” from the home page or menu bar
    2. Select whether or not you are looking for volunteer work or paid work.
    3. Filter the job list through tags, categories, dates, and descriptions.
    4. Click on a job's title to see more details
    5. Click on the "Save Job" button to save the listing
    6. Get in contact with the poster using their email or phone number
